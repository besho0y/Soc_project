Operation Chimera - Coming 2025 !

Op Chimera is a 2-week long event within the BTLO platform where players will work in teams or by themselves
to complete a number of labs and challenges all taken from the same compromise, piecing
together the actions the adversaries took by analysing logs, network traffic, security tooling, and
performing incident response.


PRO players can purchase an 'Operation Pass' to gain access to the live event, and be eligible for
physical rewards including the one-time-only metal challenge coin,
and digital rewards including
the BTLO Op Chimera badge, which will be displayed in a unique location on your profile.
There will be a new scoreboard for both individuals and teams during the event.


After the event has ended, all related content will be retired and can be accessed by PRO players for no additional fee.
